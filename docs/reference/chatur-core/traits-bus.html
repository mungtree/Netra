chatur-core / traits/bus.rs

The domain-event bus: a publish/subscribe channel, and the DomainEvent enum it carries.

Path: crates/chatur-core/src/traits/bus.rs

In plain terms

When something noteworthy happens — a job started, a batch finished — the engine announces it on the bus. Anyone interested (most importantly the desktop UI) subscribes and reacts. The publisher does not know or care who is listening.
