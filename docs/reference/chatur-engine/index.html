chatur-engine crate

The moving parts: a job queue, a scheduler, a job runner with retry, batch map/reduce orchestration, and the aggregation strategies.

What this crate is for

If chatur-core is the nouns and chatur-agent is the agent, chatur-engine is the verbs. It is where jobs actually get run: pulled off a queue, dispatched under a concurrency cap, executed against an agent with retry-on-failure, and — for batches — fanned out and recombined.

Depends on chatur-core and chatur-agent.
